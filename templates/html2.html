FDA Approval Prediction

Early and accurate identification of promising drug-related research topics can augment human judgment in resource distribution and accelerate the discovery of novel therapeutics. We leverage scientific literature data to extract predictive features to identify promising therapeutic research topics at an early stage using machine learning models.

Drug Development from the Lens of Scientific Literature

While previous approaches focused on individual trial-level prediction for drugs, we employed a scientific topic-level approach to predict promising topics to produce drug therapeutics. We subdivide the biomedical literature into research topic clusters (RC). We build a direct citation network using PubMed database and cluster the articles with high topical cohesion using modularity maximizing Leiden algorithm. Drugs are mapped to different clusters based on the position of the research articles publishing the results of the pivotal trials associated with the drugs.

Machine Learning Model for Drug Approval Prediction

we extract scientific topic-level features that hold predictive power in indicating tentative drug generation likelihood from a particular topic. We built a logistic regression model to predict the success or failure of a research cluster in generating drug intervention.
